Design For Understanding

After sifting through data sets and discussing the possibilities of exploring each, we decided on the analyzing the data provided by FiveThirtyEight on college majors. The data provided was a comprehensive set of college majors, gender makeup, salaries, employment rates, and various other data points. We split up to display the data from as clearly as possible and to create a visualization to leverage the data to convince our audience of something. Our visualizations were created with a diverse tech stack that included Vega, R, Java, and D3.js.

Design For Tension

In this Design for Tension project, we created a chatbot that attempts to tackle a tense topic. We chose to pick a concept that centers around depression as it is a tough area to deal with for those who are directly affected by it and those affiliated with the affected person. In order to narrow down the target group, we decided to aim it at teenagers who are concerned for their friends struggling with depression.

The goal of this project was to design a website that utilizes the Affectiva library to respond to a user’s emotions with the end goal of promoting wellbeing.
